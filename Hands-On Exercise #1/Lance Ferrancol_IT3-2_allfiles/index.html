Understanding Mental Health

Explore the importance of mental health and learn about common challenges people face.

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Common Mental Health Challenges

  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Depression
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Stress

Importance of Mental Health

Maintaining good mental health is crucial for overall well-being. It helps us cope with stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.
